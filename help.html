Frequently asked
How do I move a member to a different care model?
Open the member profile → Care model & plan → Move model. Billing updates from the next cycle.
How are staff payments calculated?
From approved, GPS-verified visits plus emergency duty and travel allowance. Approve in Staff Payments.
Who can see billing and payments?
Admin and Finance roles have full access; Coordinators and Management have view access.
How do I add an add-on for a member?
From the member profile Add-ons section, or the Care Models → Add-ons tab.
